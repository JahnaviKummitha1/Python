    Flow control in Python


Mainly 6 flow controls
>if-else
>Nested if-else
>For
>While
>Break
>continue

If-else:-
Syntax: 
If(condition):
              statement 1
….
       else:
              statement 2
….
 
Nested if-else:-
If (condition 1):
         statement 1….
    elif (condition 2):
           statement 2 ….
    else
            statement 3….

For:-
For iterating_var in sequence:
Execute statements

While:-
While (condition is True):
        statement1…

Break:-
It is used to break the loop

Continue:-
It wont break the loop,but will continue the loop.
